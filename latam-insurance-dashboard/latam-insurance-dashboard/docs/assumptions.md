# Assumptions & Limitations

## Data Assumptions

**Currency**  
All monetary values (premiums, density) are reported in USD millions 
as provided by OECD. No additional currency conversion was applied.

**Year Coverage**  
Data available for 2018–2022 only for Latam non-OECD countries. 
This is a limitation of the OECD Global Insurance Statistics coverage 
for this region, not a data quality issue.

**Gross Written Premiums**  
Values represent direct business only. Reinsurance accepted is excluded 
unless explicitly noted in the indicator name.

**Penetration Rate**  
Calculated by national authorities as premiums / GDP. 
GDP methodology may vary slightly by country.

**Retention Ratio**  
A high retention ratio (close to 100%) means the insurer keeps most 
risk without transferring to reinsurers. Lower values indicate higher 
reliance on reinsurance.

---

## Analytical Assumptions

**Market Tier Classification**  
Thresholds (1.5% and 3.0% penetration) are based on OECD and Swiss Re 
industry benchmarks for emerging market segmentation. These are 
approximations and may not reflect official classifications.

**YoY Growth**  
First year per country (2018) shows null growth — this is expected 
behavior as there is no prior year to compare against.

**life_nonlife_ratio**  
Where non-life premiums are zero or null, ratio is set to null 
to avoid division by zero errors.

---

## Known Limitations

- Data covers 2018–2022 only — no historical trend beyond 5 years
- ROE and investment portfolio data not available in this OECD export
- Argentina values show high volatility due to currency devaluation
